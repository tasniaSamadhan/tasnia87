About Me
Hi! This is Tasnia. I have completed my BSc. in Electrical & Computer Engineering (ECE) from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET), Bangladesh. My research interest lies in the fields of Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning and Deep Learning. I am a prospective graduate applicant. In my graduate research I want to explore the integration of Human Computer Interaction (HCI) with NLP.
